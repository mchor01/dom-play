Fish and Life

Ben: What is life?

Fish: Life is like a straight line?

Roselle: Huh?

Fish: I didn't say anything, I'm only a fish.

Ben: Nah, the fish definitely said something.

Fish: Oh you caught me.

Fish: Like begins at the beginning of the line, then ends at the end.

Roselle: That's deep.

Roselle: I view life as a curvy line, not straight.

Fish: Well from a fish's perspective, life just begins and ends, there is no meaning.

Ben: That's really sad.
